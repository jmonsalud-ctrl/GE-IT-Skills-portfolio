## `#### 2. Critical Reflection on Tool Limitations` 


"While the AI platform rapidly synthesized large volumes of tourism and
environmental literature, the audit process revealed several instances of
unsupported statistics and exaggerated claims. The tool frequently overstated
economic impacts and occasionally presented projections as established facts.
Human verification remained essential for confirming numerical data, validating
citations, and ensuring that conclusions accurately reflected the findings of
primary sources.


The review process demonstrated that AI tools are effective for accelerating
literature discovery and thematic analysis but should not be treated as
authoritative sources. Researchers must independently verify all statistics,
policy claims, and referenced studies before incorporating them into academic or
policy outputs. Combining AI-assisted synthesis with manual source validation
produced a more reliable and academically defensible literature review."
